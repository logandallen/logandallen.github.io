About Me

Logan Allen is a student at the Center for Technology Essex and is part of the Computer Animation Web Design (CAWD) program. Logan is an aspiring animator and 3D artist. Logan spends his time creating art digitally and traditionally. He is proficient in adobe programs and blender. Logan continues to work on his art outside of the classroom and enjoys creating animations and learning new things. In 2018 - 2019 Logan worked at UVM creating 3D models for prototype medical devices. Logan feels this was a good experience but wants to focus more on art-related career paths rather than engineering.

Logan Allen attended CAWD 1 in his junior year of High School. Here he learned many things about web development, graphic design, and 3D modeling. Logan enjoyed this program and field so much he went on to CAWD 2 the following year. Here he has learned skills in photography and expanded his skills within the Adobe suite. During CAWD 1, Logan was inducted into the national technical honor society and remains part of it today. He was also awarded student of the year within CAWD.
